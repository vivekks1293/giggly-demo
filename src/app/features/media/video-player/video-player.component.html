Giggly Video-player Component

The Giggly Video Player is a sleek, customizable component designed for embedding and controlling videos effortlessly within Angular applications. Built with the Giggly design philosophy in mind—playful yet professional—it ensures a polished viewing experience that fits seamlessly into your UI.

How to Use

              
                <giggly-video-player src="test.mp4" poster="C1.jpg" [autoplay]="false" [muted]="false" [loop]="false" [controls]="true"></giggly-video-player>
