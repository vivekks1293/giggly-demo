Giggly Card Component

The Giggly Card component is a flexible and stylish container designed to organize and present content in a visually appealing way. It includes dedicated sections for a header, body, and footer, allowing for structured content display while maintaining the playful yet professional Giggly theme.

How to Use

              
                <giggly-card header="Card Title" footer="Footer Text">
                <p>This is the content of the card. It can hold any custom content.</p>
              </giggly-card>
          
              <giggly-card header="Another Card" borderColor="blue">
                <p>Custom colored border with additional details inside.</p>
              </giggly-card>
              <giggly-card header="Card Title" footer="Footer Text">
      <p>This is the content of the card. It can hold any custom content.</p>
